$\omega=\sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}=\sqrt{\frac{mg/l}{m}}=\sqrt{\frac{g}{L}}$
Corresponding frequency and period functions:
$f=\frac{\omega}{2\pi}=\frac{1}{2\pi}\sqrt{\frac{g}{L}}$
$T=\frac{2\pi}{\omega}=\frac{1}{f}=2\pi\sqrt{\frac{L}{g}}$
Units: 
$\omega$ is in $rad/s$
$f$ is in Hertz/Hz
$T$ is in seconds
